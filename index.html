Karl Kilbo Edlund MD PhD

Early-career researcher-physician at the University of Gothenburg↗ and Sahlgrenska University Hospital↗

Ongoing research projects Featured publications

My research develops our understanding of how the residential and workplace environment influence our risk of chronic disease. My research interests focus on the intersection between environment, society and health. I have extensive training in epidemiological methods which I also teach at multiple levels.

Representational work within higher education and research policy has run in parallel with my professional activities for a decade. Currently, my foci include doctoral training and supervision quality, university alliances and the European Universities Initiative, early-career researcher representation, and ecological sustainability at the European level through the European Council of Doctoral Candidates and Junior Researchers↗.
