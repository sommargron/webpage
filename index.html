Karl Kilbo Edlund MD PhD

Early-career researcher-physician at the University of Gothenburg↗ and Sahlgrenska University Hospital↗

My research spans chronic disease impacts of long-term air pollution exposure and particle exposure in the workplace, health effects of other environmental exposures, and some bibliometric work. I am building a policy-related research line and am currently applying for various sources of postdoc funding at the intersection between environment, society and health. I have extensive training in epidemiological methods which I also teach at multiple levels.

Representational work within higher education and research policy runs in parallel with my training, research and clinical work, and has done so for a decade now. Current foci include quality assurance, doctoral training, and ecological sustainability, mainly at the European level through the European Council of Doctoral Candidates and Junior Researchers↗.
